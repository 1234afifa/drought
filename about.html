About Us

Welcome to DroughtInsight, your trusted source for comprehensive drought information and data analytics in India. Our platform is dedicated to providing real-time insights and actionable data to help individuals, communities, and decision-makers effectively understand, monitor, and respond to drought conditions across every state in India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower stakeholders with accurate and up-to-date information on drought-related metrics, enabling informed decision-making and proactive measures to mitigate drought impacts.

What We Offer

Real-Time Data Analysis:
Drought Percentage: We provide detailed insights into the extent and severity of drought conditions in each state, enabling users to track drought trends over time.
Crop Types: Our platform categorizes crop types across different regions, helping users understand agricultural diversity and vulnerabilities.
Rainfall Metrics: Access comprehensive data on rainfall patterns and anomalies, crucial for assessing water availability and drought resilience.
Temperature Records: Monitor temperature fluctuations and trends to gauge their impact on drought severity and agricultural productivity.
Soil Moisture Levels: Understand soil moisture dynamics, essential for predicting agricultural outcomes and water management strategies.
